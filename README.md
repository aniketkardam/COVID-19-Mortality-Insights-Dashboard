# COVID-19-Mortality-Insights-Dashboard
This project presents an interactive COVID-19 mortality analytics dashboard built to analyze death trends, high-risk groups, underlying health conditions, and geographic distribution of COVID-19 mortality.
The dashboard helps identify **which populations were most affected, how deaths changed over time, and which medical conditions contributed most to mortality risk.** The project demonstrates how data visualization can support **public health insights and data-driven decision making.**

Project Objectives

The main objectives of this project are:

* Analyze **COVID-19 death trends over multiple years**
* Identify **high-risk age groups**
* Examine **comorbidities contributing to mortality**
* Compare **state-level mortality distribution**
* Understand **temporal changes in COVID-19 deaths**

Dashboard Features
1️⃣ High-Level Mortality Metrics

The dashboard displays key summary indicators:

* **52.56M total deaths**
* **34.35M high-risk deaths**
* **23 major medical conditions** linked to COVID-19 mortality

These KPIs provide a quick overview of the scale and severity of the pandemic.

2️⃣ COVID-19 Deaths by Age Group

This visualization highlights mortality across different age groups.

**Key Insights**

* Older populations (especially **65+**) show significantly higher mortality risk.
* Younger groups such as **0-24** have comparatively lower death counts.
* Some records contain **unspecified age categories**, which are also represented.

3️⃣ Death Percentage by Medical Condition

A treemap visualizes underlying health conditions associated with COVID-19 deaths.

Major contributing conditions include:

* Circulatory diseases
* Respiratory diseases
* Diabetes
* Obesity
* Renal failure
* Sepsis
* Alzheimer’s disease

This helps identify **comorbidities that increase mortality risk.**

4️⃣ COVID-19 Death Trends by Year

A time-series chart shows yearly mortality trends.

**Insights**

* Deaths peaked during **2020**, the first major wave of the pandemic.
* Mortality gradually declined in later years as **vaccination, treatments, and public health measures improved.**

5️⃣ Geographic Distribution of Deaths

A map visualization displays **state-level mortality distribution across the United States.**

This allows users to:

* Identify **regional hotspots**
* Compare state-level mortality patterns
* Observe geographic variations in pandemic impact

6️⃣ Death Ratio by State

A comparative bar chart shows states with the highest mortality ratios.

States such as:

* California
* Texas
* Florida
* Pennsylvania
* Ohio

demonstrate higher mortality counts due to larger populations and higher case volumes.

7️⃣ Yearly Distribution of Death Groups

A donut chart displays the **proportion of deaths across different years**, helping understand how mortality shifted throughout the pandemic timeline.

Tools & Technologies

* **Power BI** – Dashboard development
* **Data Visualization** – Trend and pattern analysis
* **Public Health Analytics** – Mortality and risk assessment
* **Geospatial Analysis** – State-level mortality distribution


Key Insights

* COVID-19 mortality is significantly higher among **elderly populations and high-risk patients**
* **Underlying health conditions** play a major role in mortality outcomes
* Mortality trends **declined after 2020** as healthcare responses improved
* Geographic variations highlight the importance of **regional public health strategies**
